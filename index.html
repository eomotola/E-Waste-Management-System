Who We Are

The Waste Management System is designed to help communities responsibly handle electronic waste by reporting areas where waste is found, ensuring proper disposal, and contributing to a cleaner environment.

Our Services

What We Offer

Waste Reporting

Easily report electronic waste in specific areas through our user-friendly system.

Waste Collection

Our team works with local authorities to ensure timely collection and safe disposal of waste.

Recycling and Disposal

We collaborate with certified recycling partners to safely process electronic waste.

How It Works

Report Waste

Identify and report areas with electronic waste via our platform.

Collection

We coordinate with waste management teams to collect reported waste.

Safe Disposal

All waste is transported to certified recycling centers for environmentally friendly processing.
